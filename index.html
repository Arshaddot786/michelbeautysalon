The Salon

An atelier for the everyday client and the once-in-a-lifetime occasion.

Tucked into the Diplomatic Area, Michel Salon has spent years building a quieter kind of reputation — the kind earned one appointment at a time, not one campaign at a time. Every stylist and technician on the floor trains continuously, and every service is timed properly rather than rushed to fit a schedule.

Whether you're in for a wash-and-blow-dry between meetings or a full bridal transformation, the standard doesn't move.
